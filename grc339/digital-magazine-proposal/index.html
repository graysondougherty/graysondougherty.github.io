Introduction

Drain Gang

Drain Gang is a swedish music group known for their dream-like production and surrealist fusion of cloud rap and experimental pop. They have developed a distinct visual aesthetic characterized by a combination of hyper-surrealism, digital distortion, and ethereal imagery. The magazine will contain articles written about the members of the band, most notably Bladee and Ecco2k, as well as upcoming tour dates.
