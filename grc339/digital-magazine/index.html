DJ Billybool Drops Album, DYR

DJ Billybool, Thaiboy Digital's EDM pseudonym, has just dropped his first full Album, following his 2020 EP and subsequent singles. DYR is 10 tracks, entirely in Swedish on European dance beats. The album is highlighted by its track with Bladee and Eurohead called BLOMSTERTID.

Yung Lean Goes on Tour

Yung Lean has announced his Forever Yung Tour in support of his new album Jonatan, released on May 2, 2025. The tour begins in October across North America, with shows in cities like LA, Brooklyn, and Toronto. In November, it heads to Europe, hitting major stops including Berlin, London, and Paris.

Bladee Goes on Tour

Bladee has unveiled his 2025 Martyr World Tour, supporting his recent EP Ste the Beautiful Martyr 1st Attempt and the 2024 album Cold Visions. The tour kicks off in July with shows across Australia, then resumes in October for a North American leg, hitting cities like Brooklyn, Los Angeles, and Toronto. It concludes in December with performances in Europe and the UK, including stops in London, Dublin, and Manchester.

One in A Million Video Drops

Bladee's new video for “One in a Million” shows him drifting through a misty graveyard, sword in hand, evoking themes of martyrdom and rebirth. The track's airy production by Lusi and Woesum adds to its dreamlike, saintly atmosphere.

New Song, MAKKA

Fakemink's new single “MAKKA,” featuring Ecco2k and produced by Mechatok, blends dreamy guitar loops with punchy basslines, creating a euphoric, genre-blurring sound. The accompanying video adds a surreal, DIY charm to this underground anthem.

The End of YEAR0001

Yung Lean and Bladee have officially parted ways with their longtime label, YEAR0001. They now release music independently through their own labels—World Affairs and Trash Island—signaling a new chapter of artistic autonomy.

Bladee Drops Cold Visions

Bladee dropped Cold Visions in late 2024, a project that blends icy synths, ambient textures, and cryptic lyricism into one of his most introspective releases yet. The album marks a shift toward minimalist production, with Bladee delivering haunting melodies over sparse, glacial beats.

Bladee on Brat

Bladee appears on Charli XCX's album Brat, delivering a dreamy, genre-bending feature that contrasts her hyperpop chaos with his signature ethereal style. His verse adds a cool, detached edge to the track, bridging underground cloud rap with pop experimentalism.

Yung Lean on Brat

Yung Lean features on Charli XCX's remix of “360,” released on May 31, 2024, as part of her album Brat and It's Completely Different but Also Still Brat. His verse adds a cool, detached edge to the track, bridging underground cloud rap with pop experimentalism.

Psychos Drops

Yung Lean and Bladee released their surprise collaborative album Psykos on March 13, 2024, marking their first full-length project together after over a decade of creative partnership. The eight-track album, produced by Palmistry and silent$ky, blends alt-rock and post-punk influences with introspective lyrics, reflecting on themes of fame, mental health, and personal growth.

Cold Visions or I AM MUSIC?

Bladee's Cold Visions drifts through ambient soundscapes and glacial minimalism, channeling emotional detachment and cryptic introspection. In contrast, Playboi Carti's IAMMUSIC is raw and chaotic, bursting with distorted energy and vampiric swagger, making the two albums mirror opposites in mood and approach.

What's Next for Bladee?

Following the release of Cold Visions and the Martyr World Tour, Bladee seems poised to further explore the edges of genre and sound through solo work and collaborations under his World Affairs label. With his recent departure from YEAR0001, fans anticipate a new era of creative freedom marked by visual experimentation and unexpected musical directions.
